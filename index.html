Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Curex? ▾
Curex is a telehealth platform that connects patients with board-certified allergists who can prescribe personalized sublingual allergy immunotherapy (SLIT) drops. These drops contain the same allergen extracts as traditional allergy shots, but are taken at home under the tongue.
What conditions do you treat? ▾
Curex provides allergy care plans for allergies, asthma, eczema, and pet allergies. We treat a wide range of environmental allergies including pollen, dust mites, mold, and pet dander.
Do you treat food allergies? ▾
We do offer treatment for certain food allergies. Please take our free quiz to find out if your specific food allergy is eligible for treatment through Curex.
What is the treatment? ▾
Curex providers may prescribe sublingual allergy drops that contain the same ingredients as allergy shots but are more convenient and affordable. You take a few drops under your tongue daily for 2 minutes.
How long is the treatment? ▾
Most patients see meaningful improvement within 6-12 months, with many continuing for 2-3 years for long-lasting or permanent results.
Do you accept insurance? ▾
Yes! We accept all major insurance providers. Clinical consultations and allergy testing are billed to insurance. The monthly subscription ($59/month) covers your immunotherapy drops, shipping, and related costs.
What states do you serve? ▾
Curex is available in all 50 states. Our licensed clinicians can treat patients anywhere in the US.
Is it FDA approved? ▾
Compounded sublingual immunotherapy medications have not been individually approved by the FDA, though the individual allergen extracts used are FDA-approved. The FDA has not evaluated the safety or efficacy of compounded formulations.
How much does it cost? ▾
Getting started is only $4.99 (refundable if not prescribed). Once prescribed, treatment shipments are $39/month. Allergy testing and online doctor visits are billed to insurance.
Do you accept HSA/FSA? ▾
Yes, Curex accepts HSA and FSA payments for eligible expenses.
